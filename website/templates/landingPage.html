Take a look at our features

Symptom-Based Diagnosis

Patients can input symptoms, and the system utilizes algorithms to provide potential diagnoses, precautions, suggested medications, and information on nearby specialists.

Early Detection of Diabetes and Liver Diseases

The system analyzes patient attributes from test reports, assessing the likelihood of diabetes and liver diseases, providing prompt feedback for proactive management.

Malaria Detection through Image Analysis

Patients can upload cell images for suspected malaria cases, and image recognition algorithms accurately identify malaria parasites, facilitating rapid diagnosis and treatment planning.

Pneumonia Detection via Chest X-ray Analysis

By analyzing chest X-ray images, the solution detects signs of pneumonia, aiding clinicians in informed decision-making.

Comprehensive Health Feeds

Doctors have access to personalized feeds where they view updates, posts, photos, and other content shared by their patients. The feed algorithm prioritizes content based on health status, recent medical data, engagement with treatment plans, and doctor's preferences.

Patient Profiles

Each patient has a personal profile showcasing medical information, health history, current conditions, and treatment plans. Doctors can monitor patient health trends, track progress, and provide personalized care through these profiles.

Real-Time Health Updates and Alerts

Doctors receive real-time updates and alerts about their patients' health status, including vital signs, medication adherence, symptom changes, and appointment reminders.

Interactive Health Posts

Doctors can create and share various types of health-related content directly from their profiles or the patient feed, engaging with patients and providing guidance and encouragement.
